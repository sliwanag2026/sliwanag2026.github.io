My Experiences in the Game

Geometry Dash is probably one of my favorite games being played. Despite being around 200-300 pesos in price, I would say it is worth it. This game is really fun but can also be harmful to people with epilepsy. Anyways, I have gained a total of 320 hours in the game and I've beaten an extreme demon which is the hardest difficulty in Geometry Dash. My in-game name is Zytroph and you can search it up in the Geometry Dash search bar.
