About the Community

The Geometry Dash community is.... controversial. The community used to be very nice but as of now, it is really toxic and I suggest you don't get involved in the community. Sure there are a bunch of nice people as well, but there are people who was considered to be kind but ended up being evil.



Content Creators

There are a bunch of content creators in the gd community. Content Creators are people who make content to entertain viewers in a certain platform. Platforms include Twitch, Youtube, etc. There is also a list of content creators in this website: Content Creators
